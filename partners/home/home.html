Frequently Asked Questions

Click to Expand Question

What is the time commitment from our company?

Vivint Solar will do all of the heavy lifting. The time required by Vivint Solar from your company is minimal. We will provide you with marketing materials, a co-branded landing page, social media communications, flyers, and graphics. Your organization will send out emails and social media communications on a time table that is agreed upon by both parties. You will also be able to post flyers and display graphics promoting the partnership.

How do I get started?

To engage with us as a partner, you will need to fill out the partner information form (found here). Once we have received the form, a Vivint Solar representative will reach out to you to discuss next steps.

What markets does Vivint Solar operate in?

AZ, CA, CT, HI, MA, MD, NJ, NM, NY, PA, SC, UT

When do our customers/employees receive their money?

The installing customer will receive their incentive money within one month of installation of their Vivint Solar system.

How does Vivint Solar track the success of the campaign?

Each campaign will be given a unique landing page URL as well as a unique toll free number. Each tracking vehicle will track directly to the Salesforce campaign that has been established for your organization. Monthly reporting will be sent to each partner.

Will our company be implicated in the contract between Vivint Solar and our customer/employee?

All Vivint Solar contracts are between Vivint Solar and the installing customer.

How does going solar save my clients/employees money?

When it comes to the different ways you can save with solar, the sky’s the limit. But here are some of our favorites.

No Up-Front Costs We take care of the upfront costs. Sizing, procuring, and installing your solar energy system won’t cost you a dime. That means you start saving money as soon as the system becomes operational.

Reduced Energy Costs With GO, you save money every month on your energy bill with a fixed energy rate that’s more affordable than your utility company.

Limited Risk We’re responsible for maintaining and repairing your solar energy system.

Potential Increase in Property Value GO has been shown to increase property value. That makes it a pretty great home investment at no cost.

How long will it take to get the solar energy system installed and running?

Great question. And that depends largely on your local utility. We usually install each solar energy system within a week after we receive your permit from the city. But because it’s tied to the grid, your local utility company has to inspect your system and turn it on. That can take a few days, or a few weeks or months depending on the utility. While this is completely out of our control, we’ll do everything in our power to expedite the process.
